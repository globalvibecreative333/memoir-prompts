7 ChatGPT Prompts to Start Telling Your Life Story

Use your voice. ChatGPT will help you find the words.

For Your Own Story
1
"I want to start telling my life story but I don't know where to begin. Ask me one question at a time about a memory that felt like a turning point for me. Keep your questions simple and conversational."
2
"Ask me about the place I grew up — what it looked like, what it felt like, and how it shaped who I am. Take it slow and let me think out loud."
3
"I want to talk about someone who influenced my life in a big way. Ask me questions about who they were, what they did, and why they still matter to me."
4
"Ask me about a time in my life when everything changed — a decision, a loss, a move, a moment. Help me tell that story out loud."
For Capturing Someone Else's Story
5
"I want to interview my [mother/father/grandparent] about their life. Help me start with warm, easy questions that won't feel like an interview. One question at a time."
6
"Ask me questions I can bring to a conversation with an older family member about what life was like when they were young — their neighborhood, their family, their daily life."
7
"Help me capture a specific story my [family member] has told before but never written down. Ask me what I remember about it and help me fill in the gaps."
